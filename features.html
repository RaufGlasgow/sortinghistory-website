Features

Everything you need for solo learning, family game nights, and classroom competitions. No multiple choice. No repetitive questions. Just pure timeline trivia.

Not Your Typical Trivia Game

Tired of picking A, B, C, or D? Sorting History uses a timeline mechanic where you drag historical events into chronological order. It's more engaging, more challenging, and you actually learn something.

Each round shows you a timeline and asks you to place a new event. Get it right, and your timeline grows. Get it wrong, and you learn the correct placement. Either way, you walk away knowing more than when you started.

Three Ways to Play

Solo Adventure

Challenge yourself. Progress through historical events, earn ranks from F to S+, and track your mastery across all categories. Perfect for learning at your own pace or competing against your personal best.

Works offline, so you can play on planes, trains, or anywhere without WiFi.

Pass & Play

Family game night on one device. Teams take turns sorting events. Perfect for road trips, waiting rooms, or any time you want to see who knows history best.

Supports 2-8 players split into teams, with customizable team names.

Network Play

No more passing the phone. Connect multiple devices over local WiFi and compete in real-time. Each player uses their own device, with synchronized scoring and turn order.

Great for classrooms, parties, and family gatherings. Available to all players, including free accounts.

You Control the Challenge

Most trivia apps have one difficulty: frustrating. We give you three scoring modes so everyone from beginners to history buffs can enjoy the game.

Friendly Mode

No penalties. +1 for correct, 0 for wrong. Perfect for learning, kids, or anyone who wants to enjoy history without pressure.

Competitive Mode

Balanced challenge. +2 for correct, -1 for wrong. Rewards knowledge while keeping mistakes recoverable.

Expert Mode

For serious history buffs. +3 for correct, -2 for wrong. High stakes, high rewards. Prove you really know your stuff.
