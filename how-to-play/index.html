How to Play Sorting History

Sorting History is a timeline trivia game where you put historical events in chronological order. Choose a guide below to learn the rules, set up a game, or sharpen your strategy.

Game Modes

Sorting History

The core game. Place historical events on a growing timeline in the right order. Three difficulty levels, 5 to 30 rounds, 12 categories with 100+ events each.

Learn the rules →

Daily Challenge

A new 4-event sorting puzzle every day in a surprise category. Same events for every player worldwide. Under 5 minutes. Available on all tiers.

History Pinpoint

Guess the exact year a historical event happened. Four precision levels, optional hints. Available to Historian subscribers.

Quick Start: Your First Game in 60 Seconds

  1. Open Sorting History and tap Play on the main menu.
  2. Pick a mode: Sorting History (timeline) or History Pinpoint (year guessing).
  3. Choose Solo, Pass & Play, or Network Play.
  4. Select your categories. Start with one you know well, or mix several for variety.
  5. Set your difficulty. Friendly mode has no penalties — great for your first game.
  6. Pick a game length. Sprint (5 rounds) takes under 3 minutes.
  7. Play! In Sorting History, place each event where it belongs on the timeline. In History Pinpoint, type your best guess for the year.

Or skip setup entirely: tap the Daily Challenge card on the main menu for a quick 4-event puzzle that changes every day.
