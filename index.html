Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sorting History?

Sorting History is a history trivia game for iPhone and iPad where you sort historical events in chronological order. It's designed for both solo players and groups, with 30 categories covering everything from ancient civilizations to modern pop culture.

Can I play alone or do I need other people?

Both! Solo Adventure mode lets you play alone and earn ranks based on your accuracy. Team Battle and Network Play modes let you compete with family, friends, classmates, or coworkers.

Is Sorting History free?

Yes, Sorting History is free to download and play.

What devices does Sorting History work on?

Sorting History runs on iPhone and iPad with iOS 15.0 or later.

Is this game good for families with kids?

Absolutely! Sorting History is designed to be family-friendly and educational. It's a great way for kids to learn history while having fun with parents and siblings. The Friendly scoring mode removes penalties for wrong answers, making it perfect for younger players.

Can teachers use this in classrooms?

Yes! Network Play mode lets you connect multiple iPads for classroom competitions. Teachers can select specific categories that align with their curriculum and adjust game length to fit class periods.
