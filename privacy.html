1. Information We Collect

Data Type When Collected Purpose

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name (unless you tell us)

  • Your browsing history outside the App

2. Information Stored Locally

The following stays on your device only and is never sent to us:

  • Player name (display in game)
  • Game scores and history
  • Streaks and achievements
  • App preferences
  • Subscription status (synced with Apple, not our servers)

To delete this data: Uninstall the App or reset it in iOS Settings.

3. Advertising and Tracking (Ad-Supported Only)

This section applies ONLY if you use the free, ad-supported version.

If you subscribe to Explorer or Historian, skip this section — we disable all advertising and no ad-related data collection occurs.

What Ads You'll See

Ad Type When How Often
Interstitial (full-screen) Between games Max 1 per game session
Rewarded video Your choice When you opt in

Our Advertising Partner

We use Google AdMob to show advertisements. When you use the ad-supported version, AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA): To personalize ads (you decide via iOS prompt)
  • IP address: For approximate location
  • Device info: To serve compatible ads
  • Ad interaction data: To measure ad performance

For details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

Before any personalized tracking, iOS will ask your permission:

  • "Allow" = Personalized ads based on your activity across apps
  • "Ask App Not to Track" = Non-personalized (contextual) ads only

Either way, you still see ads on the free tier. The difference is whether ads are personalized.

Change this anytime: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

How to Stop All Ad Tracking

Method What Happens
Subscribe to Explorer or Historian All ads disabled, all ad tracking stops
Deny ATT permission Ads continue but are not personalized
Limit Ad Tracking (iOS) Reduces personalized ads system-wide

Subscribing is the only way to eliminate ads entirely.

8. Local Network Usage (Multiplayer)

The App includes optional local multiplayer that uses your Wi-Fi network.

What It Does What It Doesn't Do
Discovers nearby devices running the App Send data over the internet
Shares game state between devices Connect to our servers
Works without internet Track your location

iOS will ask for "Local Network" permission. This is optional and only affects multiplayer.

10. Children's Privacy

Sorting History is suitable for all ages, including families with children.

For children under 13:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • We recommend parents subscribe to Explorer or Historian to provide an ad-free, tracking-free experience
  • The ad-supported tier shows contextual ads (not targeted to children specifically)

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, contact us at privacy@sortinghistory.com and we will delete it.
