Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 5, 2026

Sorting History ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Sorting History is designed to be privacy-friendly. We collect minimal information:

  • Bug Reports: If you choose to submit a bug report, we collect the information you provide (description of the issue) along with basic device information (device model, iOS version, app version) to help us diagnose and fix problems.
  • Email Address: Only if you voluntarily provide it when submitting a bug report or contacting support.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, location, or personal identifiers
  • We do not track your gameplay or create user profiles
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

Local Data Storage

All game progress, scores, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed from your device.

Network Features

Sorting History includes local network multiplayer features (Team Battle and Network Play). These features use Apple's local networking frameworks to connect devices on the same Wi-Fi network. No game data is transmitted over the internet during local multiplayer sessions.

Children's Privacy

Sorting History is designed to be family-friendly and educational. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation, and all gameplay data remains on the device.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us through a bug report, please contact us at privacy@sortinghistory.com and we will delete that information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect any information you provide. Bug reports are transmitted securely and stored in private systems with limited access.

Third-Party Services

Sorting History does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media SDKs. The app may be distributed through the Apple App Store, which has its own privacy policy available at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any bug report data you've submitted
  • Request information about what data we have associated with your email address
  • Opt out of communications by contacting us

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
